# FAQ ### what is 3 degrees? 3 degrees is a network of matchmakers and their single friends. ### how does it work? 1. If both singles agree with the match, they can start chatting within the app to set up a date . ### Can any random person see the singles' profiles? Nope. Matchmakers have to give each other permission to view their singles. We want singles to feel secure in their privacy. ### Seems like the best way to make matches or get "proposals" is to grow my network. How do I do that? Easy, for both singles and matchmakers: just send an invite from the app using your phone contacts list. ### what is the inspiration behind 3 degrees? 3 degrees is inspired by the Yale Freshman Screw, which is a campus-wide event where roommates set each other up on blind dates. ### how does it differ from other dating sites? The biggest difference is that 3 degrees is very matchmaker-driven. Singles play a secondary role, which a lot of people find refreshing. ### Are we talking about professional matchmakers here? Nope. Anyone can be a matchmaker. Generally though, we find that the people who want to be matchmakers are those already in relationships. ### Why would matchmakers put in the time to set up their friends? A 2014 study that came out of Harvard and Duke found that matchmakers are happier than non-matchmakers. Matchmaking is also a big part of many cultures. We think that most people would be willing to put in a small bit of time in order to help out their friends or family who haven't been so lucky in the modern dating scene. ### How come singles can't browse other singles? We really want to keep the ball in the matchmaker's court. This seems to take a lot of pressure off of the date. ### Why do the singles have to be 3 degrees apart? Other services link up singles via one matchmaker. We think this type of service is unnecessary because that's basically real life. If a person knows two singles who may make a good match, that person can probably just shoot both singles an email introduction. By using the networks of 2 matchmakers, the singles now have a larger, more varied pool of potential mates. ### Can a single also be a matchmaker? Currently our program can only support one type of version per phone. This may change in the future.
