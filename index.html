Who We Are

For years, we've felt that one of the most rewarding things we could do for our friends would be to help them find love. We've all been there: you want to set up a friend on a date, but that friend already knows everyone you know - and has already dated half of them. There are only so many new single co-workers that walk into our lives. We built 3degrees as a solution to this problem. We love using the app to help our friends find love - and hope you do too.
